String Band

String Band

I direct rehearsals and arrange for a small community of amateur musicians based in New Orleans. This recent activity began in Summer 2020 as a way for a small group of friends to stay connected during the pandemic lockdowns. My work in this genre has became more formalized since I began holding regular rehearsals in Spring 2022 at the church where I work. Since that time, I've started collaborating with musicians from the intergenerational New Orleans community. I've also produced more notated arrangements to aid rehearsal directing.

I have been performing, composing and arranging for a guitar-based ensemble since my middle-school days circa 2007. From 2008 to 2011, I composed and performed with Jacob Hartranft, Matthew Lidell and Kevin Thomas as Traveling Cadavers in Rockville, MD. I spent my college years doing solo singer/songwriting. I occasionally facilliated songwriting circles, most successfully around 2014.
