String Band

String Band

I have been involved in music direction and arrangement for a group of amateur musicians in New Orleans since 2020. This began as a way for a group of friends to stay connected during the pandemic lockdowns, and has since become more formalized with regular rehearsals at the church where I work. In addition to collaborating with musicians from the local community, I have also created more notated arrangements to help with rehearsal direction.

My experience in music composition and performance dates back to my middle school years, where I played guitar in an ensemble. From 2008 to 2011, I composed and performed with Jacob Hartranft, Matthew Lidell, and Kevin Thomas as part of the Traveling Cadavers in Rockville, MD. During my college years, I focused on solo singer/songwriting, and also occasionally facilitated songwriting circles, with the most successful being in 2014.
