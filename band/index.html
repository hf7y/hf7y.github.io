String Band

String Band

I have been involved with a group of amateur musicians in New Orleans as a music director since 2020. Our open-membership group plays a balance of mid-century folk rock revival and millennial alternative rock. We hold regular rehearsals at the church where I work as the music director. I provide sheet music, and have also created notated arrangements to help with rehearsal direction.

The string band began as a series of informal sing-alongs among my friends. Seeing the musical talent within the group, I recognized the potential to create something more meaningful. Drawing on my experience and background as a musician, I stepped up to help bring out the best in everyone. When I started working at the church, I expanded the group to include the wider community. My goal is to make music accessible to anyone who wants to learn and play together.

My experience in music composition and performance dates back to my middle school years, where I played guitar in an ensemble. From 2008 to 2011, I composed and performed with Jacob Hartranft, Matthew Lidell, and Kevin Thomas as part of the Traveling Cadavers in Rockville, MD. During my college years, I focused on solo singer/songwriting, and also occasionally facilitated songwriting circles, with the most successful being in 2014.
