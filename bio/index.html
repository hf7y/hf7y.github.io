Bio

Zachary Viet Pine is a versatile musician based in New Orleans, with a background in composition and sousaphone performance. He has a passion for incorporating spiritual elements into his music, drawing inspiration from his time in Berkeley and his uncle's Zen practice. Zach's compositions often feature a wide range of instrumental techniques and may incorporate digital manipulation and quotation. He has studied with renowned composers Max Dulaney and Rick Snow, and his work has been performed by esteemed ensembles such as Mivos Quartet and Ensemble LINEA.

In addition to his work as a composer, Zach serves as the Music Director at the First Unitarian Universalist Church of New Orleans. In this role, he leads hymns and directs the choir, frequently creating arrangements or compositions for liturgical use. Zach is dedicated to exploring the transformative power of music in the context of spirituality and community.
